About Advocate Sunil Panwar

"A man without any ego, and a person who always helps others."

Advocate Sunil Panwar is an esteemed and highly respected pillar of the legal community at the Baghpat District Court. With more than 20 to 30 years of extensive experience as an advocate, he has successfully navigated and argued some of the most complex legal battles in the region, always maintaining an absolute dedication to truth and justice.


Having served with high distinction as the District Government Counsel (DGC) Criminal, Adv. Panwar possesses an unparalleled, deep-rooted understanding of criminal proceedings, evidence laws, and judicial prosecution. He has built a legendary reputation not just on his consistent courtroom victories, but on his fiercely ethical practice, deep knowledge of the Indian Constitution, and unbreakable client trust.


Beyond his vast professional accolades, Adv. Panwar is deeply revered throughout the district and the legal bar association for his incredibly humble nature. He is known as a guardian of the law who stands unwaveringly dedicated to assisting those in need, ensuring that justice remains accessible, fair, and fiercely defended for the common man, regardless of their background.
